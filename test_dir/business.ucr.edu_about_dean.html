Welcome from the Dean

 

It is my pleasure to welcome you to our website, where you can learn more about the UCR School of Business and its various programs.

As part of the University of California (UC) system, the School of Business is committed to providing a quality business education at a world-class research university. Our students learn from distinguished scholars and practitioners who have unmatched academic credentials among international leaders.

We are proud to be ranked among the Top 100 Best Business Schools in the US. The pursuit of excellence in education is the standard at the UCR School of Business, and to that end, we continually strive to build an environment that inspires collaboration and empowers our students, faculty, and alumni to succeed. These goals drive our strategic decisions and how we shape the future of business education.

As a student with us, you will learn to identify and evaluate opportunities for new and growing ventures, and how to manage and sustain opportunities for growth in organizations, industries, markets, and communities. You will grow personally and professionally as business people, managers and community leaders. And most importantly, you will be driven by a passion for discovery and innovation.

The School of Business is committed to greater engagement and playing an impactful role in the economic and social landscape of the regional and worldwide communities we serve.

Yunzeng Wang
School of Business Dean

 

 

 

About Dean Wang

Yunzeng Wang was named dean of the UCR School of Business in January 2014 after serving as the interim dean since October 2011. He first joined the School of Business faculty in July 2008 as the Dean’s Distinguished Scholar in Supply Chain Management and Professor of Finance and Management Science. He has also served as Associate Dean of Academic Programs. Dean Wang has brought exceptional vision and leadership to the UCR School of Business, taking it to new levels of excellence.
