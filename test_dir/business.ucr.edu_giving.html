Make an Impact

The UCR School of Business plans to increase graduate school enrollment, increase the size and enhance the quality of its faculty, and launch new research centers. These centers will include entrepreneurial leadership, economic forecasting, and supply chain management and logistics.

To accommodate current and future growth, the School of Business will be breaking ground on a new state-of-the-art building complex to house its programs and centers.
