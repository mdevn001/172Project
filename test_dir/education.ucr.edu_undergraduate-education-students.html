Welcome!

Welcome to the School of Education’s undergraduate advising office. As you navigate your undergraduate studies, our student support staff are here to answer questions you may have about university policy and procedures, provide academic advice, and help you achieve your academic goals.

We encourage you to review the resources we’ve compiled on this page. Information includes community engaged learning & fieldwork, SOE undergraduate writing support, enrollment deadlines, important forms, processes, and procedures. Our team is available for advising appointments in-person and virtually. If you're an incoming student, you can find orientation information here.

SOE Writing Support

SOE's writing support coordinator Delaney Rood is available to assist SOE students through every step of the writing process and general student success, including understanding prompts, brainstorming, and time management. Appointments available weekly, between 9 a.m. - 1 p.m.

Visit the Writing Support Page to Book an Appointment
