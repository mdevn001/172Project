The UCR School of Public Policy (SPP) is the latest addition to the UC Riverside academic enterprise. The Master of Public Policy program was launched in Fall 2015, and continues to train a new generation of forward-thinking public policy leaders equipped to address the complex, interrelated challenges of poverty, disease, climate change, pollution and more. The School of Public Policy includes the Dean's Office and five research centers: the Center for Sustainable Suburban Development, the Center for Social Innovation, the Presley Center for Crime and Justice, the One Health Center which is part of the UC Global Health Institute, and the Blum Initiative for Global and Regional Poverty.
