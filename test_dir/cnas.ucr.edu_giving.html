Supporting CNAS

Central to our research excellence is educating the students who come to the College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences (CNAS) to learn and leave with an integrated grasp of how they can change the world. These students, and the faculty who teach them, benefit from a structure that is unique among land-grant colleges: CNAS's 13 departments encompass the life, physical, mathematical, and agricultural sciences.

Donor support has a direct impact on CNAS students, empowering them to delve into research, stay curious, and prepares them to fill the gaps in the job market as research-minded scientists.
