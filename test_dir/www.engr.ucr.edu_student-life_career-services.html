GIVING YOU THE TOOLS
TO SUCCEED IN YOUR ENGINEERING CAREER

No matter where you are in your journey, the UCR Career Center provides guidance and resources that help you successfully prepare for and transition to a career in engineering.

Student Services

  • Resume and cover letter building
  • Career exploration
  • Major choice counseling
  • Graduate/professional school planning
  • Interview skills workshops
  • Employer information sessions
  • Career panels
  • Specialized job discovery events
  • Job and internship fairs
  • Curated job and internship board via HandShake

Visit the Career Center

Alumni Services

  • One year of access to all Career Center services, except on-campus interviews with employers
  • One year of access to Handshake
  • One year of access to workshops, job fairs and other events
  • Additional services after one year available for a fee
  • UCR Alumni Association members receive a 20% discount on all such services

"Access to UCR’s robust industry network gave me invaluable career connections that helped me secure a job before graduation."

Samuel Dominguez, B.S. Computer Science, 2020
Software Engineer, VMWare

MEET THE TEAM

VANESSA LEE

Career Specialist, Graduate Students & International Students
vanessa.lee@ucr.edu

Vanessa Lee serves as the Graduate Student Career Specialist and has been with UCR Career Center since 2014. She supports all graduate students in finding their career paths, and collaborates with Industry Outreach Specialists in identifying the best opportunities to serve our graduate student population. With a background in vocational psychology and career development, Vanessa has been working in this field for 10+ years. She graduated with a Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology from the University of Minnesota’s (UMN) Psychology Department, and received her BA in psychology at UMN. Vanessa also serves as the Career Center’s International Student Career Specialist.

XIN FAN

BCOE Career Specialist, Undergraduate Students
monica.cruz@ucr.edu

Xin Fan serves as the BCOE Career Specialist for undergraduate students. She provides career counseling for BCOE students sand supports BCOE students in exploring career planning and developing career readiness beyond their engineering education. Xin is the alumna of Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering, UC-Riverside. She graduated from UC-Riverside with her Ph.D. degree in Chemical and Environmental Engineering. As an alumna of BCOE, Xin Fan has strong enthusiasm and passion to help BCOE undergraduate students launch their future career feeling confident.
