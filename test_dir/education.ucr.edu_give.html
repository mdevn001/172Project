Give

Donors to UC Riverside’s School of Education support scholarships and fellowships for graduate and teacher credential students, as well as faculty research, centers, and chairs within the SOE. A gift to SOE can take the form of a one-time gift toward a current fund, or a gift to an endowed fund that pays annually for generations. We also welcome planned gifts that can benefit you and your loved ones at this time, and UCR in the future. Your generosity will assure the ongoing development of excellent teachers, professors, administrators and academic change makers—shaping the quality of education for generations to come.

If you have any questions, please contact Kelly Kraus-Lee, Director of Development, (951) 543-7342.
