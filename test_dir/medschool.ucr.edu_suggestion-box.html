School of Medicine Online Suggestion Box

The suggestion box may be used to provide constructive suggestions and ideas to improve the UC Riverside School of Medicine.

  • In the spirit of continuous quality improvement, proposed solutions to any suggestion that involves a problem or difficulty are encouraged.
  • Anonymous submissions may not generate a public response but will still be read and acted upon when feasible.
  • Comments or suggestions that use offensive language may be discarded.

The online form below may be used to submit your suggestion. You may submit the form anonymously, but providing your name and email address will allow us to acknowledge you for sharing your thoughts with us and give us the opportunity to follow-up with you.

You may also submit your suggestion by email by sending it to suggestions@medsch.ucr.edu.

Students may send education-specific recommendations to pamela.hunter@medsch.ucr.edu.

Innovation Program Suggestions

If you would like to submit a suggestion to the School of Medicine Innovation Program, please visit their site at this link. (You must be on campus or using a VPN to access this site.)
