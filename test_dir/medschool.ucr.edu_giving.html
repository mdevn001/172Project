Supporting the Future of Medicine

Breadcrumb
  •  
  • Supporting the Future of Medicine

Your philanthropic support provides the UCR School of Medicine with the resources and tools necessary to support our students, research, clinical services and capital projects – all benefiting SOM’s mission of providing excellence in healthcare to diverse and underserved patient populations.

There are several ways that you can make a gift to the School of Medicine. Click the left button below to see a list of SOM-specific funds, or use our secure app form at the bottom right to make an immediate gift using a credit card.

UCR staff and faculty who want to make a gift through monthly payroll deduction can download the PDF by clicking the right button.

All gifts will allow the School of Medicine to continue in our commitment to train the next generation of physicians, advance groundbreaking research, and provide extraordinary clinical care.
